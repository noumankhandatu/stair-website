How do I save my design and get a CAD drawing?
Click the 'Email Quote' button in the bottom right of the screen and your design and quote will be sent to you, along with a link to place an order for this design should you wish to.
I've processed my order online, but there are a couple of changes I need to make, can I change this online?
Unfortunately not - you’ll need to call us so that we can make sure that no work has been done on the flight and so we can make the alterations immediately so as not to delay your order.
How accurate are the sizes on the Brad Joinery?
All staircases are manufactured to the exact sizes shown on the Brad Joinery, however as timber is a natural product it can swell slightly in different temperature and humidity conditions (usually around 5mm in the width). We always recommend leaving an additional 10mm surrounding your staircase for fitting purposes. We advise you to double check the CAD drawing which you will receive within 24 hours of placing your order.
Will my staircase comply with Building Regulations?
Anything designed using our Brad Joinery will comply with UK Building Regulations as dictated by Document K for Domestic purposes. Should you require a staircase that complies with Commercial Regulations, or outside Building Regulations, please call us.
I would like to buy multiple staircases but your Brad Joinery doesn't provide a discount.
Please get in touch with us via phone or email and we’ll look into the best price that we’re able to offer you.
Can you provide online help when using the Brad Joinery?
Yes, we offer live support during office hours. Simply click the "Live help" button in the bottom left of the screen and follow the onscreen instructions.
